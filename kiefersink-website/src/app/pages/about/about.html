About Us

More Than Ink

At Kiefer’s Ink Tattoo, we believe every tattoo is more than ink — it’s an expression of individuality. We specialize in custom designs, fine-line detailing, and bold traditional pieces, blending precision with personality in every stroke. Whether you’re coming in for your first tattoo or adding to a growing collection, we work closely with you to capture the meaning behind your design and craft something that’s uniquely yours.

With a clean, relaxed studio environment and a passion for the craft, we make sure your experience is as unforgettable as the art itself.

Artists. Storytellers. Creators.

Our artists are passionate storytellers with needles and ink, each bringing a unique style, steady hand, and years of experience. From bold traditional work to intricate realism, they pour creativity and precision into every design, ensuring each piece reflects both artistry and authenticity.
