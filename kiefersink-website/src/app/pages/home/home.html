Crafting Iconic Tattoos & Piercings Since 2016

Since 2016 in Pasig, Kiefer’s Ink Tattoo has been a trusted spot for both tattoos and piercings. What began as a small passion project has grown into a studio known for authentic designs, creative expression, and a commitment to safety and hygiene.

Whether you’re getting your first piercing or a full-sleeve tattoo, our artists bring your story to life with skill and care.

Our Services

Tattoos

From minimalist designs to full-sleeve masterpieces, our artists specialize in bringing your vision to life with clean lines, bold colors, and expert technique.

Piercings

We provide safe, hygienic piercings for ears, nose, and beyond. With a wide range of high-quality jewelry, we ensure your piercing experience is as stylish as it is comfortable.

Our Works

Our gallery showcases the passion, precision, and creativity behind every piece we craft. From delicate linework to bold full-color art, each design reflects our artists’ dedication to turning ideas into lasting impressions.
